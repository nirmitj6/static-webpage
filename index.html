About

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Northwestern University advised by Julia Gaudio and Aravindan Vijayaraghavan. I also work with Nati Srebro at the Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago. My research focuses on the mathematical foundations of machine learning and optimization, espcially in deep learning theory, random graph theory, and federated learning.

Previously, during my undergradutate studies, I have also done research in streaming algorithms with Sepehr Assadi.
